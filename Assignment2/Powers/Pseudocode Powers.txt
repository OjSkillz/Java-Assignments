Pseudocode : Powers

Stage 1:
1. Prompt and collect two whole numbers 
2. Raise either numbers to the power of the other.
3. Display results.

Stage 2:

1. Prompt user to input two whole numbers
2. Collect both numbers
3. Store as "x" and "y" respectively
4. Prompt user to select either of two options 1 and 2 where:
	1 is "Type "A" to raise x to the power of y
    and 2 is "Type "B" to raise y to the power of x
5. Store selection as "response" 
6. If response is A, raise x to the power of y while 
	
7. If response is B, raise y to the power of x
8. Display results based on selection of user.

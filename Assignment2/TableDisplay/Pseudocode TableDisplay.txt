Pseudocode: Display A Table

Stage 1:
1. set counter to begin at 1
2. initialize a at 0
3. initialize b at 1
4. print table header using the horizontal tab escape sequence to specify column width
5. while counter is less than 6 and incrementing counter by 1,
	increment a by 1
	increment b by 1
6. Print results of a, b and a raised to the power of b under the corresponding headers using the tab escape sequence.

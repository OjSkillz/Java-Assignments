Pseudocode : Sum Of Natural Numbers

Stage 1:

1. Prompt and Collect first 10 natural numbers
2. Calculate the sum of the first 10 natural numbers
3. Display result

Stage 2:

1. Set counter of natural numnbers to begin at 1
2. Set sum to begin at 0
3. Prompt user to enter the first ten natural numbers beginning with 1
4. While the natural number is less than or equal to 10, let sum be equal to sum + the natural number while also incrementing the counter of natural numbers by 1.
5. At the end of the inputs, display the sum of the first 10 natural numbers.

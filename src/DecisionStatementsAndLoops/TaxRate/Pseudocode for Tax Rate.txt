
Pseudocode  for Tax Rate Indicator

Prompt user to enter their annual income
Collect and store input as annualIncome 
	If annualIncome is less than or equal to  $9875
		Print "0 - $9,875: 10%"
	Else if annualIncome is between $9876 and $40,125
 		Print " $9,876 - $40,125: 12%"
 	Else if annualIncome is between $40,126 and $85, 525 
		Print  "$40,126 - $85,525: 22%"
	Else 
		Print "$85,526 and above: 24%"
